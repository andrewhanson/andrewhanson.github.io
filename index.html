My Manifesto

"Mayhem" - sometimes that's how I would describe my days. Especially in business. If you know my story then you know that I've always just kind of found my way through my freelance software career for the last 7 years. It's been great but it's been anything but organized.

Several times during the last month I've found myself sharing my 4 pillars of being freelance software developer.

  1. Share what you know
  2. Be the community
  3. Contribute your code
  4. Manage your cash flow
Honestly though, I haven't been following my own pillars very well lately. So I've write them down into a personal Manifesto Writing things down makes them real and telling other about them makes them 'realer'.
